# Property Catastrophe Treaty Guidelines

Florida hurricane or windstorm exposure requires underwriter referral when the cedent has material coastal concentration, significant homeowners exposure, or incomplete ZIP-level exposure data.

Minimum attachment for catastrophe treaty business should generally be reviewed when below $10M, especially for hurricane, wildfire, earthquake, or flood-exposed layers.

Any wording conflict where a peril appears both covered and excluded must be treated as a critical referral item before pricing or quote indication.

Large-limit placements of $100M or more require accumulation review against the current portfolio and historical loss experience.

Submissions with missing schedules of values, unclear perils, missing exclusions, or low extraction confidence should be routed for manual review.
